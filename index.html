About Dr. Manu Banga

Dr. Manu Banga is an Assistant Professor at the School of Engineering & Sciences, GD Goenka University, Sohna, Haryana, India.

With over a decade of academic experience, he has taught at renowned institutions, including GLA University Mathura, JC Bose University Faridabad, and Amity University Rajasthan.
